Frequently Asked Questions

What does an intellectual property lawyer do?

An intellectual property attorney helps individuals and businesses protect their creations, inventions, and brand identities. This includes filing for patents, registering trademarks and copyrights, drafting licensing agreements, and litigating against infringement.

Why should I hire an Irvine intellectual property lawyer?

Working with a local Irvine intellectual property lawyer ensures you have an advocate who understands both federal IP laws and California-specific business regulations. We provide accessible, in-person consultations and have deep knowledge of the Orange County business landscape.

How long does it take to register a trademark?

The trademark registration process typically takes between 8 to 12 months, assuming there are no major objections or oppositions. Our firm ensures your application is filed correctly the first time to minimize delays.

Do you handle business litigation outside of IP disputes?

Yes. While we are highly regarded for our IP practice, Jafari Law Group also handles a wide array of general business litigation matters, including breach of contract, fraud, shareholder disputes, and real estate litigation.

What employment law services do you provide?

We represent clients in matters involving workplace discrimination, sexual harassment, wrongful termination, wage and hour violations, and retaliation. We also assist businesses in drafting compliant employee handbooks and employment contracts.
