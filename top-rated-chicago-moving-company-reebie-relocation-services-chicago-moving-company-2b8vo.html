Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your upcoming move in the Chicago area.

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend contacting your Chicago moving company at least 4 to 6 weeks prior to your desired move date. Summer months and ends of the month are peak seasons, so booking earlier ensures availability.

Do you provide packing and unpacking services?

Yes, Reebie offers comprehensive packing and unpacking services. Our expert team uses high-quality materials to secure your fragile items, electronics, and everyday belongings for safe transport.

Are my belongings insured during the move?

Absolutely. We provide basic liability coverage with all moves, and we offer comprehensive valuation options so you can have complete peace of mind knowing your items are protected.

What areas do you serve?

We are proudly based in Chicago, IL, and serve the entire Chicagoland area. In addition to local moves, we handle long-distance, interstate, and international relocations.

How do you estimate the cost of a move?

Moving costs are typically based on the distance of the move and the total weight or volume of your belongings. We provide transparent, in-home or virtual estimates to give you an accurate and competitive quote without hidden fees.
