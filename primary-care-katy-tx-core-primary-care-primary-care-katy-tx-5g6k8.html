Frequently Asked Questions

What services are included at your Primary Care Katy TX clinic?

We provide a wide range of medical services including preventive care, routine physicals, chronic disease management, immunizations, and acute illness treatment for patients of all ages.

Are you currently accepting new patients at the Katy location?

Yes, Core Primary Care is currently accepting new patients at our Katy clinic. We welcome individuals and families seeking compassionate, personalized healthcare from experienced providers.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your valid photo ID, current insurance card, a list of any medications you are currently taking, and any relevant past medical records to help us provide the best care possible.

How often should I schedule a routine check-up?

We generally recommend an annual wellness exam for most adults. However, depending on your age, medical history, and specific health needs, your physician may suggest more frequent visits to monitor your health.

Do you treat and manage chronic conditions like diabetes?

Absolutely. Our experienced medical team specializes in chronic disease management. We work with you to monitor your condition, adjust medications, and recommend lifestyle changes to improve your overall quality of life.
