Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide emergency roof repairs in Toronto?

Yes, we understand that roof leaks and storm damage don't wait for business hours. We offer prompt roof repair services to address urgent issues and prevent further damage to your property.

What types of flat roofing systems do you install?

We specialize in various modern flat roofing systems suitable for Toronto's climate, including Modified Bitumen, EPDM, and TPO. Our experts will recommend the best solution based on your building's specific needs.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

For a standard residential home, a roof replacement usually takes 1 to 2 days. However, the timeline can vary based on the size of the roof, weather conditions, and the complexity of the job.

Are your roofing contractors licensed and insured?

Absolutely. The Roof Technician is fully licensed, insured, and WSIB compliant. We prioritize safety and professionalism on every job site to give you complete peace of mind.

Can you replace an existing leaking skylight?

Yes, we are experts in both new skylight installations and replacing old, leaking, or damaged skylights. We ensure proper flashing and sealing to guarantee a watertight fit.
