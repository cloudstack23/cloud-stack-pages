Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your heavy duty fire pits different from store-bought ones?

Mass-produced, store-bought fire pits are typically made from thin, cheap metals that rust through and fall apart after a year or two. Our heavy duty fire pits are handcrafted by an American manufacturer using thick, premium steel. They are built around durability and function, designed to be a long-term, multi-generational investment for your home.

Do you offer accessories to match the fire pits?

Yes! We manufacture a full line of custom accessories designed to perfectly fit our fire pits. This includes heavy-duty snuffer lids to safely extinguish fires, spark screens for added safety, cooking grates for outdoor grilling, and matching firewood racks.

Where are your products made?

All S&S Fire Pits products are proudly handcrafted in the United States. Our manufacturing facility is located at 2411 Sullivan Rd., College Park, GA, where we utilize old-school craftsmanship and American-made materials.

Can I leave my fire pit outside year-round?

Absolutely. Because we build heavy duty fire pits from thick steel, they are designed to withstand the elements. Over time, they will develop a beautiful, natural patina that adds to their timeless design without compromising their structural integrity.

Do you make anything besides fire pits?

Yes, we offer a variety of outdoor lifestyle products. In addition to our core fire pit line, we handcraft custom steel planter boxes, premium gas log sets, and other functional outdoor decor built with the same rugged durability.
