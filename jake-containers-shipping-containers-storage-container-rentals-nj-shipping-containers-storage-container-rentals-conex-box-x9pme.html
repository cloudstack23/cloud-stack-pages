Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our storage solutions and delivery process.

What sizes of containers do you offer?

We primarily offer standard 20-foot and 40-foot shipping containers. We also provide high-cube options which offer an extra foot of vertical clearance for taller items or increased volume.

Are your containers weatherproof and secure?

Yes. All of our units are inspected to ensure they are Wind and Water Tight (WWT). They are constructed from heavy-duty corrugated steel and feature secure locking mechanisms to protect your belongings.

Do you deliver to my location?

Absolutely. We provide delivery services throughout the state of New Jersey and the greater Tri-State area. Delivery costs are calculated based on your specific location and the size of the unit ordered.

Is it better to rent or buy a container?

This depends on your timeline. If you need storage for less than a year, renting is typically more cost-effective. For permanent or multi-year storage needs, purchasing a container is usually the better investment.

Do I need a permit to keep a container on my property?

Permit requirements vary strictly by municipality. We highly recommend checking with your local zoning or building department in New Jersey before scheduling a delivery to ensure compliance with local regulations.
