Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency plumbing services in Albuquerque?

Yes, we understand that plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours. If you have a burst pipe, severe leak, or overflowing drain, contact us immediately for prompt assistance.

How often should I have my HVAC system serviced?

We recommend servicing your HVAC system twice a year—once in the spring for your A/C unit and once in the fall for your furnace. Regular maintenance helps prevent unexpected breakdowns and extends the life of your equipment.

Are your plumbers and technicians licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Every member of the 505 Plumbing team is fully licensed, trained, and insured to perform plumbing, heating, and cooling work safely and up to New Mexico state codes.

What areas do you serve?

Do you provide estimates before starting work?

Yes, we believe in transparent pricing. Once we diagnose the issue, we will provide you with a clear, upfront estimate before any work begins so there are no surprises on your final bill.
