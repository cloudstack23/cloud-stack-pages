Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our optometry services in Houston, TX.

How often should I visit an optometrist in Willowbrook?

We generally recommend that adults schedule a comprehensive eye exam with an optometrist in Willowbrook every year. However, if you have existing eye conditions, diabetes, or wear contact lenses, you may need more frequent visits. Children should have their first eye exam at 6 months, again at age 3, and before starting school.

Do you offer pediatric eye care?

Yes! Quality Eye Care provides comprehensive pediatric eye care. Our caring team is experienced in making children feel comfortable while we assess their visual development, check for refractive errors, and ensure their eyes are healthy for school and play.

What types of contact lenses do you provide?

We offer a wide curated selection of contact lenses, including soft daily disposables, bi-weekly and monthly lenses, toric lenses for astigmatism, multifocal lenses, and rigid gas permeable (RGP) lenses. During your fitting, we will determine the best option for your visual needs and comfort.

Can you treat eye diseases like glaucoma or macular degeneration?

Absolutely. Our practice is equipped with advanced diagnostic technology to detect, manage, and treat various eye diseases. We provide ongoing care for conditions like glaucoma, macular degeneration, diabetic eye disease, and dry eye syndrome to help preserve your vision.
