Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide at your Willowbrook eye care center?

We provide a full spectrum of vision care services including comprehensive eye examinations, custom contact lens fittings, pediatric vision care, and the diagnosis, management, and treatment of various eye conditions.

Where is Quality Eye Care located?

Our clinic is conveniently located at 7540 Cypress Creek Pkwy, Houston, TX, 77070, United States. We proudly serve the Willowbrook area and surrounding Houston communities.

Do you diagnose and treat eye conditions?

Yes, our dedicated team of optometrists is highly trained in the diagnosis and treatment of numerous eye conditions, including dry eye syndrome, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy.

At what age should my child have their first eye exam?

We recommend that children have their first comprehensive eye exam at 6 months of age, again at age 3, and right before starting kindergarten. Our pediatric vision care services are designed to be comfortable and engaging for children.
