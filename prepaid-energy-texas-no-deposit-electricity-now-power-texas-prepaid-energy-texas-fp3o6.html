Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our prepaid energy Texas services.

What is prepaid energy?
Prepaid energy, also known as pay-as-you-go electricity, allows you to purchase your electricity before you use it. Instead of receiving a bill at the end of the month, you load funds into your account, and the cost of your daily usage is deducted from your balance. It gives you complete control over your energy spending.
Do I need to pay a deposit to start service?
No! At Now Power Texas, we never charge a deposit. To get started, you simply make an initial payment to fund your account balance, and 100% of that money goes toward the electricity you will use.
Will you check my credit score?
Absolutely not. We do not require an ID or a Social Security Number to run a credit check. Because you are paying for your power in advance, your credit history is completely irrelevant. Everyone is approved.
How fast can I get my electricity connected?
If your home has a smart meter (which most homes in deregulated Texas do), we can typically get your power connected on the exact same day you sign up, often within just a few hours.
How do I add funds to my account?
We make it easy to recharge your account. You can add funds online through our secure portal, over the phone, or in person at thousands of payment center locations across Texas. We will send you daily text or email alerts so you always know your balance.
