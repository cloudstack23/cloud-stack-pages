Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with a professional concrete contractor.

What services does a professional concrete contractor provide?

As a full-service concrete contractor, we handle everything from site preparation and grading to pouring, finishing, and sealing. Our specialties include driveways, patios, sidewalks, stamped concrete, retaining walls, and custom sport courts.

How long does a new concrete driveway last?

When properly installed and maintained, a high-quality concrete driveway can last 25 to 30 years or more. We use premium mixes and proper reinforcement techniques to ensure maximum longevity and resistance to cracking.

Do you offer stamped and decorative concrete options?

Yes! We specialize in stamped concrete, offering a wide variety of patterns and colors that can mimic the appearance of natural stone, brick, slate, and even wood. It's an excellent way to add luxury to your property at a fraction of the cost of natural materials.

How soon can I walk or drive on my new concrete?

Generally, you can walk on new concrete after 24 to 48 hours. However, you should wait at least 7 days before driving standard vehicles on a new driveway, and up to 28 days for heavy machinery, as concrete takes time to fully cure and reach its maximum strength.

How do I maintain my concrete patio or driveway?

Concrete is relatively low maintenance. We recommend keeping it clean from debris, washing it periodically, and applying a high-quality concrete sealer every 2 to 3 years to protect it from moisture, stains, and freeze-thaw damage.
