Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of motorized garage storage lifts?

The primary benefit of motorized garage storage lifts is safety and convenience. They eliminate the need to climb ladders while carrying heavy boxes. By utilizing overhead space, you clear your floor for vehicles and workspaces, all operated easily via a wall switch or smartphone app.

Which brands of storage lifts do you carry?

We proudly offer top-tier brands known for their reliability and innovation, including Auxx Lift, Garage Smart, and Ceiling Sam. Each brand offers unique features to suit different ceiling heights and weight requirements.

Do you offer solutions for Jeep hard tops?

Yes! We carry specialized hard top lifters designed specifically for Jeeps and trucks. These systems allow a single person to safely hoist the roof off the vehicle and store it securely against the ceiling.

What types of outdoor sheds are available?

We supply premium outdoor sheds, including durable vinyl and fully insulated models from trusted brands like Duramax. These sheds are weather-resistant, require minimal maintenance, and provide excellent security for your outdoor equipment.

How do I choose the right storage solution for my home?

Choosing the right solution depends on your ceiling height, the weight of the items you plan to store, and your available floor space. We recommend measuring your space and reviewing the weight capacities of our lifts and sheds on our website to find the perfect fit.
