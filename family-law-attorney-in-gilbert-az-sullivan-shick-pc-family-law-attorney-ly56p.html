Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really need a lawyer for my divorce?
While it is possible to file for divorce on your own, having an experienced attorney ensures that your rights are protected, assets are divided fairly, and paperwork is filed correctly to avoid future complications.
How is child custody determined in Arizona?
Arizona courts determine custody based on the "best interests of the child." Factors include the relationship between parent and child, the child's adjustment to home and school, and the mental and physical health of all parties involved.
Why should I hire a Family Law Attorney?
Hiring a qualified Family Law Attorney provides you with an objective advocate who understands the complex legal system, helping you make informed decisions during emotionally difficult times.
