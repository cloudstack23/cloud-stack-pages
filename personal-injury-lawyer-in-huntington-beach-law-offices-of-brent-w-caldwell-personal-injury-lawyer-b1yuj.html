Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about personal injury claims in Orange County.

When should I hire a personal injury lawyer?

It is highly recommended to consult with an attorney as soon as possible after your accident. Early intervention allows us to preserve crucial evidence, speak to witnesses while memories are fresh, and prevent insurance companies from taking advantage of you.

How much does it cost to hire your firm?

We work on a contingency fee basis. This means you pay zero upfront costs and no out-of-pocket fees. We only get paid if we successfully recover compensation for you through a settlement or verdict.

What types of cases do you handle in Orange County?

We handle a wide spectrum of personal injury cases, primarily focusing on motor vehicle accidents (car, truck, motorcycle), premises liability (slip and falls), dog bites, catastrophic injuries, and wrongful death claims.

How much is my personal injury case worth?

The value of your case depends on several factors, including the severity of your injuries, total medical expenses, lost earning capacity, and the level of pain and suffering. During your free consultation, we can evaluate the specifics of your situation to provide a clearer picture.

Will my case have to go to trial?

The majority of personal injury cases are settled out of court through negotiations with the insurance company. However, if they refuse to offer a fair settlement, we are fully prepared to take your case to trial to fight for the compensation you deserve.
