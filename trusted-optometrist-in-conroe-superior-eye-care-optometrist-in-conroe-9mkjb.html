Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our practice and vision care services.

How often should I visit an optometrist in Conroe?

We recommend that adults have a comprehensive eye exam every 1 to 2 years, depending on age, risk factors, and whether you currently wear glasses or contacts. Children should have their first exam at 6 months, again at age 3, and before starting school. To schedule your next visit, contact Superior Eye Care today.

Do you offer a wide selection of eyeglasses and sunglasses?

Yes! Our optical boutique features a wide selection of designer frames, prescription eyeglasses, and premium sunglasses to suit every style and budget. Our experienced staff will help you find the perfect fit for your face shape and visual needs.

What should I bring to my eye exam appointment?

Please bring your current eyeglasses, contact lenses (and boxes if possible), a list of any medications you are taking, and your vision insurance information. This helps your optometrist in Conroe provide the most accurate and personalized care.

Can you treat dry eye syndrome?

Absolutely. Dry eye is a common condition that can cause significant discomfort and blurred vision. We offer comprehensive evaluations to determine the underlying cause of your dry eyes and provide customized treatment plans to restore comfort.

Are you accepting new patients in Conroe?

Yes, Superior Eye Care is always welcoming new patients! We are proud to serve families throughout the Conroe area. You can easily book an appointment online or by calling our office during business hours.
