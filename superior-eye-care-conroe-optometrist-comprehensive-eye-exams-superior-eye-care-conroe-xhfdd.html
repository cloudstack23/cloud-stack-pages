Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our practice and eye care.

What should I expect during a comprehensive eye exam?

During your visit to Superior Eye Care Conroe, our optometrists will review your medical history, measure your visual acuity, test your eye coordination, and perform a thorough examination of the internal and external structures of your eyes to check for any signs of disease.

How often should I get my eyes checked?

We generally recommend that adults and children have a comprehensive eye exam every year. However, if you have existing eye conditions, diabetes, or a family history of eye disease, our doctors may recommend more frequent visits.

Do you offer contact lens fittings for hard-to-fit eyes?

Yes! We specialize in fitting patients with astigmatism, presbyopia, and other conditions that make wearing contacts challenging. We offer a variety of specialty lenses to ensure comfort and clarity.

Do you diagnose and treat eye diseases?

Absolutely. Our experienced optometrists are trained to diagnose and manage a wide variety of eye conditions, including dry eye syndrome, glaucoma, macular degeneration, diabetic retinopathy, and eye infections.

What types of eyewear do you carry?

Our optical center features a diverse selection of high-quality frames from top designers, budget-friendly options, children's eyewear, and prescription sunglasses. Our opticians will help you find the perfect fit and style.
