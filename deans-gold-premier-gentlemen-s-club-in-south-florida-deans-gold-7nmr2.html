Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before visiting.

What is the dress code?
We enforce a smart-casual dress code to maintain our upscale atmosphere. Collared shirts and neat attire are recommended. No athletic wear, excessively baggy clothing, or work boots are permitted.
What is the minimum age for entry?
All guests must be 21 years of age or older to enter. A valid, government-issued photo ID is required at the door.
Do I need to make a reservation?
While walk-ins are always welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation for VIP bottle service, bachelor parties, or large groups to ensure the best possible experience.
Is parking available?
Yes, we offer convenient parking for our guests. Valet service is also available during peak hours for your convenience.
Can I host a private event here?
Absolutely. We specialize in hosting private events, corporate gatherings, and bachelor parties. Contact our management team to discuss customized packages.
